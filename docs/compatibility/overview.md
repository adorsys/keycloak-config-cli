# Compatibility Overview

The `keycloak-config-cli` is designed to work with various versions of Keycloak and its enterprise equivalent, Red Hat Single Sign-On (RH-SSO). Since Keycloak evolves over time, some configuration options, features, and behaviors may vary depending on the Keycloak version you are running.

This section covers the compatibility matrix, known limitations, and guidelines for ensuring a stable integration.

- [Keycloak Versions](keycloak-versions.md) - Details on supported Keycloak versions and their mapping with keycloak-config-cli.
- [Red Hat SSO Compatibility](../docker-helm/RedHat-SSO-compatibility.md) - Specific guidelines for RH-SSO deployments.
- [Limitations](limitations.md) - Known bounds, unsupported features, and workarounds.
